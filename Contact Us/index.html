Sweet iced Coffee with Coconut Milk

access_time

TOTAL TIME 10 minutes

workspace_premium

LEVEL 10 minutes

wallet

BUDGET Under $2

Sweet iced Coffee with Coconut Milk is easy to make and stores well in the fridge. Make a large batch and save yourself a few trips to the coffee shop.

INGREDIENTS

  • check_box 3 tablespoon Espresso coffee powder
  • check_box_outline_blank 1 cup of boil water
  • check_box_outline_blank 1 cup coconut milk
  • check_box_outline_blank 1/2 cup of sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a small bowl, combine the espresso coffee powder and boiling water. Stir until the coffee is dissolved.
  2. Boil some water and let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Put the coffee grounds into a cafetiere and pour over the hot water.
  4. Stir the coffee and water together and leave to brew for 5 minutes.
  5. Press the plunger down slowly to separate the coffee grounds from the liquid.
  6. Pour the coffee into a glass and add the coconut milk and sugar.
  7. Stir the coffee and milk together until the sugar is dissolved.
  8. Add ice cubes to the glass and serve.
